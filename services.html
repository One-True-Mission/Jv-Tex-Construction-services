Site Preparation

Proper site preparation is essential to the success of any construction project. We offer comprehensive site prep services to clear, level, and ready your site for construction. From land clearing, grading, and demolition to drainage solutions and foundation preparation, our skilled team ensures your site is safe and stable for building.

Land Clearing Grading Foundation Prep Demolition

Excavation

JVTEX.CO is ready to tackle any project that requires precise digging and earth movement. We provide excavation for foundation digging, trenching, landscaping, septic system installation, and more. Our team uses modern equipment to dig, level, and move earth with precision.

Foundation Digs Septic Systems Utility Trenching Landscaping

Trenching & Backfilling

Efficient trenching and backfilling for utility installation, foundation work, or other site preparation needs. Our experienced team uses the right equipment and techniques to safely complete your project, backfilling with quality materials for long-lasting stability.

Utility Lines Quality Backfill Compaction

Drainage Systems

Proper drainage protects your property and ensures long-term structural integrity. We design and install custom drainage systems tailored to your specific needs. We assess your site to implement effective solutions that prevent water damage and minimize flooding.

French Drains Surface Drainage Erosion Control

Retaining Walls

Professional retaining wall construction to enhance both the functionality and aesthetics of your property. Whether for soil stabilization, erosion control, or creating tiered landscapes, we build durable, attractive walls designed to last.

Soil Stabilization Tiered Landscapes Erosion Control

Demolition

Safe and efficient demolition for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Whether you're renovating, clearing land, or removing outdated structures, our team uses modern equipment to safely tear down and remove unwanted structures, including selective demolition that preserves the surrounding property.

Selective Demo Full Tear-Downs Debris Removal

Concrete

Expert concrete services for residential and commercial projects. We pour, mix, and finish high-quality concrete for foundations, driveways, sidewalks, patios, and more. Whether installing a new slab or repairing existing surfaces, we deliver a smooth, durable finish.

Foundations Driveways Patios Slabs
