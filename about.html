About JVTEX.CO

One family. One number. Every step of the project.

JVTEX.CO LLC was established in 2021 in the small town of Dale, Texas. As a family-owned business, we are committed to the growth and success of every project we put our name on.

Our founder, Jaime A. Vizcaino, brought seven years of operational experience to life when he founded the company. With a clear vision, Jaime aimed to create a reliable and versatile business that turns your needs and aspirations into reality.

That vision is simple: be the one phone call a builder, contractor, or homeowner can make to get the dirt work done right, the first time.
